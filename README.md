# NEPSIS
NEPSIS: Reclaiming human attention through "Technical Watchfulness." A Linux-based gateway that uses AI to audit AI. Filters sectarian noise, scams, and addictive loops at the router level. Built for the Lebanese home with Java, Python, and PostgreSQL.

Project Name: NEPSIS
Acronym: Network Ethical Protection for Sovereign Independent Streams
Lead Developer: Yorgo Bekaii
Primary Stack: Linux (OS), Java (Gateway), Python (Intelligence), PostgreSQL (Registry), Flutter/Kotlin (App)
1. Executive Summary & Vision
NEPSIS is an enterprise-grade home security gateway designed to solve the modern crisis of Attention Fragmentation. In the current digital economy, attention is the primary currency, exploited by multi-billion dollar companies through "Engagement Maximization." When unexamined, AI and recommendation systems stop being tools and become "dangerous masters," eroding human autonomy and amplifying anxiety.
The vision of NEPSIS is to democratize the ethical right to a safe digital environment. By moving the defense from the device to the Network Level, we provide a "Sovereign Home" solution that protects users from misaligned AI, predatory social engineering, and the "silent logic" of addictive algorithms.
1.1. The Root: Nepsis and Eastern Orthodox Watchfulness
The core of this project—and the reason for the name—is found in the Eastern Orthodox tradition of Nepsis (νηψις).
In the Philokalia and the writings of the Desert Fathers, Nepsis is defined as Watchfulness or Sobriety of Mind. It is the practice of standing as a "watchman" at the gates of the heart, discerning which thoughts (or logismoi) are allowed in and which must be rejected.
The Theological Root: Human development requires stillness (hesychia). You cannot achieve stillness if your mind is a crowded marketplace of digital notifications.
The Project Link: NEPSIS is the technical implementation of this spiritual discipline. It acts as the "watchman" for the home, filtering out the intrusive "digital thoughts" (manipulative ads, biased news, addictive algorithms) before they can enter the domestic sanctuary.

2. Technical Implementation Hierarchy (By Priority)
Phase I: Sovereign DNS Gateway & Hardening (Infrastructure)
The foundation is a Linux-based DNS Filter that operates at the network's entry point, making it "un-deletable" by local device tampering.
DNS Interception: Uses Linux iptables to force all DNS requests through the NEPSIS Java Engine, defeating attempts by users to manually change DNS settings.
VPN & Proxy Defense: Implements port blocking and protocol identification to prevent the use of unauthorized VPN tunnels.
Mainstream Utility: Blocks adult content, gambling, and malicious domains for the entire household with zero configuration on individual devices.
Lebanese Context: Provides a "Political Mute" toggle to filter out known sectarian or inflammatory local news domains.
Security: Implements DNS-over-HTTPS (DoH) to prevent ISP-level tracking and tampering.
Phase II: The "Ethical Auditor" (Python NLP Logic)
This layer adds intelligence to the raw filtering. It treats AI and news not just as data, but as Active Agents influencing choice.
Bias & Suggestibility Detection: Uses Python-based Natural Language Processing to scan content streams. It flags "Suggestible" headlines that use emotional priming or dehumanizing language common in Lebanese media.
AI Misalignment Guard: Acts as a middleware for LLM and chatbot interactions, identifying when an AI is using "therapeutic" manipulation or biased logic.
Deepfake & Scam Defense: Monitors for patterns associated with Facebook-based social engineering and AI-generated scam videos to protect vulnerable users.
Phase III: The "Attention Audit" Dashboard (User Agency)
To move from "Restriction" to "Empowerment," NEPSIS provides a transparent look at the "Attention Currency" spent.
Visual Analytics: A dashboard showing the ratio of "Productive/Educational" traffic versus "Engagement-Maximized" traffic.
Autonomy Score: Helps users understand how much of their digital time is spent on intentional actions versus algorithmic redirections.
Phase III.I: The NEPSIS Mobile Command Center (Cross-Platform App) 
To ensure mainstream adoption, NEPSIS utilizes Zero-Touch Provisioning (ZTP).
Hardware-App Handshake: The physical NEPSIS Gateway (Raspberry Pi/dedicated hardware) utilizes mDNS and Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) to automatically pair with the user’s smartphone upon plug-in.
One-Tap Activation: Through the NEPSIS Mobile App, users can activate the "Home Shield" with a single tap. The system automatically handles network redirection (via DHCP hijacking or ARP management), removing the need for the user to manually configure complex router DNS settings.
Management Interface: The app serves as the human-facing "Remote Control," allowing parents to toggle "Lent Mode," view the "Attention Audit," and receive real-time alerts on blocked AI scams or deepfakes.
Device Mapping: Assign specific "Shield Levels" to individual devices (Parent vs. Child) via MAC address identification.
Un-deletable Sovereignty: Unlike traditional parental controls, the protection is enforced at the network level; deleting the app from a child's phone does not disable the gateway's protection.
Phase IV: Specialized Modes (Lent & Asceticism)
Rooted in the Eastern Orthodox tradition of Nepsis (Watchfulness), this mode is for users seeking deep focus and technical discipline.
Lent Mode: An automated "Digital Fasting" schedule. During specific periods, the system automatically throttles "Infinite Scroll" platforms and limits the network to educational, theological, and essential communication streams.
Theological Integration: Provides "Virtue Prompts" instead of generic block pages, grounding technical discipline in ancient wisdom.
Phase V: Enterprise Integration & Organizational Scalability 
In its final phase, NEPSIS scales into a B2B (Business-to-Business) solution for Lebanese organizations. By deploying a "Centralized Focus Gateway," companies can protect their workforce from "Information Fatigue" and the rising threat of deepfake-driven corporate espionage. This expansion transitions NEPSIS from a domestic protector to an organizational standard for digital integrity, reclaiming employee attention as a strategic asset.
2.1. Implementation Strategy & Professional Stack
The OS Layer (Linux/Debian): Serves as the Hardened Environment. NEPSIS is built to run on a lightweight, secure Linux distribution. This allows for direct control over the kernel's networking stack, enabling "Zero-Touch" features like DHCP management and hardware-level traffic redirection that are impossible on standard consumer OSs.
The Gateway (Java): Acts as the high-performance Infrastructure Layer. Java is chosen for its enterprise-grade stability and ability to handle high-concurrency network traffic with minimal latency, ensuring the "Shield" does not slow down the user's internet.
The Brain (Python): Powers the Intelligence Layer. Python’s industry-standard NLP libraries allow for the real-time ethical auditing of "Independent Streams," identifying sectarian bias and AI-driven emotional manipulation.
The Registry (PostgreSQL): Chosen as the #1 demanded database in the Lebanese and global tech markets. It provides the robust, scalable data integrity required to manage thousands of unique family accounts and "Sovereign Rules."
The Interface (Flutter/Kotlin): Provides a Mainstream, Cross-Platform mobile experience that masks technical complexity behind a "Hekma-driven" UI, allowing for one-tap control of the entire network.
